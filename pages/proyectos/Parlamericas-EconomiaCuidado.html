About the project

All individuals require care at some point in their lives. It is a fundamental pillar of the economic and social system in which we live, yet many tasks associated with it do not receive the recognition they deserve.

In late 2022, we created several graphic recordings during the Parliamentary Network Meeting (Parlamericas), capturing the discussions surrounding the care economy. Each country presented a report on its local situation, persistent challenges, and progress made.
