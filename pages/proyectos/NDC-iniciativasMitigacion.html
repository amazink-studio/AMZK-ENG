Climate change mitigation initiatives – NDC

> Animation and storytelling

About the project

Climate change adaptation, mitigation, and the pursuit of sustainable development are global goals. Colombia has undertaken various commitments in support of these goals, establishing a set of regulations and incentives for projects and initiatives that contribute to achieving them.

In a series of 9 videos, we explained which initiatives and projects can benefit from these incentives, how they should align with each other and with the nation, and the parameters they must consider to be part of this shared vision.
